OPERATOR

Operators

are responsible for collecting payments from patrons frequenting a gas station. Accept payments for gas, food, medicine, cigarettes, or alcohol and deliver change. Fill patrons gas tanks if necessary. Working with money is hard. You can count wrong and it will cost you some money. Add to it gasoline and you will have a quite difficult physical and mental work. And gas stations need to work at night too. After 18 months I was so tired that I couldn't think clearly. So it was time to go.
